About Us

A look into world of Denim Plus Lifestyle

Denim Plus Lifestyle Pvt Ltd. is founded in year 2021 by Engineering graduates who saw a dream to flourish fashion industry for youngsters with vibrant attire. Since the day of our establishment we pour all our excitement into the fabrics and reflect the spirit of this excitement within our products which we offer as ready–made clothing. Our focus is truly inspired by Made In India and Vocal For Local concept.
